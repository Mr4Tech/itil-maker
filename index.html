How to Play

  • Flip two cards at a time.
  • Match each ITIL concept with the correct paired card.
  • Correct match: +100 points.
  • Wrong match: -15 points.
  • Peek penalty: -50 points.
  • 100-card mode uses the full 50-pair deck.
